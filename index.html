// about

From the classroom to the console

I've spent time since 2018 teaching math — breaking down ideas that look intimidating on paper until they're not. Somewhere along the way I started doing the same thing with code: turning "how does this even work" into "oh, that's it?"

Now I'm building front-end projects and coding curriculum, mostly landing somewhere in between: I like tools that teach, and lessons that feel like tools.

I'm based in Egypt, working with teams remotely, most recently exploring coding and Scratch instruction with a UK-based team.
